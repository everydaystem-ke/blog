Overview

Everyday STEM is a recently launched education program dedicated to increasing accessibility and enhancing STEM teaching to children across Kenya.

Why Now?

In today’s world of ever-evolving world innovation and rapid technological leaps it has become important for everyone to keep up and familiarize themselves with them. Just like writing and communication has become an integral skill necessary for participation in society at every level, so will technological literacy in the near future. Exposure to these technologies and methods from primary and secondary levels will equip a student with important skills that will be vital for innovation and critical thinking in whatever field they end up in. Thus ensuring a quality education.

Operations

Our operations cover two targeted spheres of students. The first sphere is within institutional schooling, in which we offer custom, independently run programs to schools in line with their desires and needs. The second is a program designed for home-schooled children that helps parents find an environment that addresses the challenges they face in the instruction of their children in STEM, such as finding hands-on experiments, projects, and resources. Our programs are charged per student.
